GEP Completion Checklist — General Education Program (36 credit hours)

▼
How the GEP works: You must complete 36 total credit hours across five Florida State Foundations. In each foundation you must complete at least 2 courses, and at least one must be a Florida state core (♦) course. You may choose up to two additional courses from any foundation to reach 36 hours. Click each box below when completed.
Communication Foundation ✓
Min. 2 courses · at least 1 ♦
Required: ENC 1101 (♦) and ENC 1102. Additional options: COM 1000, SPC 1603C, SPC 1608, or a course for which ENC 1101 is a direct prereq. Min. grade of C- for SWR courses.
Humanities Foundation ✓
Min. 2 courses · at least 1 ♦
♦ options: HUM 2020, MUL 2010 (SWR), PHI 2010, THE 2000 (SWR). Also: ARH 2050, ARH 2051, EUH 2000 (SWR), EUH 2001 (SWR), FIL 1000, FIL 2030, HUM 2210 (SWR), HUM 2230 (SWR), LIT 2110 (SWR), LIT 2120 (SWR), MUH 2017, MUH 2019, MUH 2111, MUL 2016, MUL 2720, REL 2300, WOH 2012 (SWR), WOH 2022 (SWR).
Mathematics Foundation ✓
Min. 2 courses · at least 1 ♦
♦ options (SMR): MAC 1105C, MAC 2311C, MGF 1130, STA 2023. Also: COP 2500C, MGF 1131, STA 2014. MAC 1105C and STA 2023 require a C or better for most Rosen programs.
Social Sciences Foundation ✓
Min. 2 courses · at least 1 ♦
♦ options: AMH 2010 (SWR,CL), AMH 2020 (SWR,CL), POS 2041 (CL), ANT 2000, ECO 2013, PSY 2012. Also: ECO 2023, HSA 2117, SYG 2000. ECO 2013 or ECO 2023 is a major requirement for most Rosen programs (C or better).
Natural Sciences Foundation ✓
Min. 2 courses · at least 1 ♦
♦ options: AST 2002, BSC 1005, BSC 2010C, CHM 1020, CHM 2045C, EVR 1001, PHY 2020, PHY 2048, PHY 2053. Also: ANT 2511, CHM 1032, CHS 1440, GEO 1200, GEO 2370, GLY 1030, MCB 1310, PHY 1038, PSC 1121.
State Writing Req. — 4 SWR Courses ✓
4 SWR courses total · min. grade of C- each
Approved SWR courses: ENC 1101, ENC 1102, MUL 2010, THE 2000, EUH 2000, EUH 2001, HUM 2210, HUM 2230, LIT 2110, LIT 2120, WOH 2012, WOH 2022, AMH 2010, AMH 2020.
Civic Literacy Requirement ✓
1 CL course + State Civic Literacy Exam
Step 1: Complete AMH 2010, AMH 2020, or POS 2041 (all qualify as CL courses).
Step 2: Pass the State Civic Literacy Exam. Both steps are required to satisfy the Civic Literacy requirement.
5 Florida State Foundation Courses (♦) ✓
One ♦ course in each of the 5 foundations
You must complete at least one Florida state core (♦) course in each foundation: Communication (ENC 1101 required), Humanities, Mathematics, Social Sciences, and Natural Sciences.
